My name is Jorge Luis Ortiz Diaz, JLOrtizDiaz for short. I've been writing ever since I can remember. Manifesting stories from thin air and having multiple conversations with myself at a time.

Saying that out-loud sounds much crazier than I actually am... I think.

And, as you can see, I am easily distracted. Not important to the subject, I just may lose track at some point. You have been warned. Now welcome to my stories.
I don't consider my stories to be necessarily impactfull, but I do want you to understand where they come from. In middle school, I was not a popular chap. Being picked on was just a daily challenge that pushed me to the utmost edges of my mind. There, I would find peace and solace in the stories playing out. Imagine a puppet show, but one where every moment changes dynamically and new stories created on the fly.
